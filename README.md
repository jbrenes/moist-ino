\# moist-ino 🌿💧



`moist-ino` is a minimalist, automatic home plant watering system powered by Arduino. It monitors soil moisture levels and delivers the perfect amount of hydration to keep your plants thriving, ensuring you never under-water or over-water them again.



The project is designed to be lightweight, clean, and highly efficient—giving your plants a perfect sip exactly when they need it.



\---



\## ✨ Features



\* \*\*Real-time Monitoring:\*\* Continuously tracks soil moisture using a capacitive/resistive moisture sensor.

\* \*\*Smart Irrigation:\*\* Automatically activates a 5V/12V water pump when soil moisture drops below a defined threshold.

\* \*\*Minimalist Architecture:\*\* Clean, highly commented, and optimized Arduino code (`.ino`).



\---



\## 🛠️ Hardware Requirements



To build your own `moist-ino` setup, you will need:



\* 1x Arduino Board (Uno, Nano, or Micro)

\* 1x Soil Moisture Sensor (Capacitive recommended for longevity)

\* 1x 5V/12V Mini Submersible Water Pump

\* 1x 5V Relay Module (or MOSFET switch)

\* 1x External Power Supply (appropriate for your pump)

\* Jumper wires and tubing
